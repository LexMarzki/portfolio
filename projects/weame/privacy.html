Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 February 2026

Weame (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Weame does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The app does not access your contacts, store WhatsApp conversations, or track your precise location.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores generated phone numbers and optional messages locally on your device for history purposes.

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not uploaded to any server
  • Is not shared with us
  • Can be cleared anytime using the “Clear All” feature inside the app

3. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

This information is used to provide relevant ads and improve advertising services.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We do not collect or store personal data on external servers. All generated history remains on your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Weame is not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Clear stored history anytime within the app
  • Reset your advertising ID in your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
